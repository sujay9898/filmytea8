Our Story

Founded in 2024, FilmyTea began as a passion project by a group of cinema enthusiasts who wanted to bring the artistry of movie posters into modern homes and spaces.

We believe that great movies deserve great art, and that art deserves to be accessible to everyone. Our carefully curated collection spans decades of cinema, from timeless classics to contemporary masterpieces.

Every poster in our collection is selected for its artistic merit, cultural significance, and ability to transform any space into a personal cinema gallery.
